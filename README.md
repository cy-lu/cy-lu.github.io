# Chun-Yu Lu – Academic Website

Source files for my academic website:

https://cy-lu.github.io

This site collects teaching material, computational examples, and technical notes used in courses and research activities related to computational physics and engineering simulation.

## Teaching Focus

The teaching modules emphasize connecting **physical theory, numerical methods, and practical simulation workflows**.  
Examples include topics such as:

- computational electromagnetics
- transfer matrix method 
- computational structural mechanics
- classical laminate theory
- numerical methods for physics and engineering

Many pages include **working MATLAB codes and simulation examples** designed to help students move from theory to implementation.
