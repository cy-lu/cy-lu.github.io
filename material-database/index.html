Mathematical model definitions

Rational-pole (RP)

εRP(z) = ε∞ + Σj=1N [ rj/(z − pj) + rj*/(z − pj*) ].

Here z is the normalized complex-frequency variable, pj is a complex pole, rj is the residue, and N is the number of pole pairs.

Critical-point (CP)

εCP(ω) = ε∞ + Σj=1N [ cjΩj/(Ωj − ω − iΓj) + cj*Ωj/(Ωj + ω + iΓj) ].

Ωj is the transition frequency, Γj is the damping rate, and cj is a complex oscillator coefficient. The public CP entry reports the best CP-family branch.
